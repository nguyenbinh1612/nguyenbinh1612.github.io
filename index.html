ART

Before and at the same time as my astronomy career, I also work as a writer, a translator, a graphic artist, while also dabbling in photography and music. My main focus is poetry; I write poems in Vietnamese, English and French with a combination of both free verse and classical forms (sonnets, lục bát, alexandrines, to name a few). Between 2019 and 2021, I also worked on a translation of the medieval Vietnamese novel-in-verse Tale of Kiều (Truyện Kiều) into English heroic couplets.
